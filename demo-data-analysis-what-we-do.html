Vulterabilities

Vulnerability Scanning

Our Vulnerability ScanningVulnerability Scanning service ensures that your frontend code remains secure by integrating SonarQube into your Dev, Test, or Prod environments for continuous monitoring. The system automatically scans your code, detects security vulnerabilities, and sends real-time notifications to your team for prompt action. Along with detailed reports highlighting security flaws, including OWASP Top 10 and CWE vulnerabilities, we provide an additional service of creating user stories and tasks against these findings, ensuring they are tracked, prioritized, and resolved efficiently. This service helps maintain a history of security fixes while aligning with best practices.
